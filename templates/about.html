About VPlus

VPlus is an app built for the Virtual Enterprise program, and is for educational purposes only.
VPlus is designed as a firm directory app, designed for companies all around the world to connect at the touch of a bottom. By doing so, firms are able to conduct business with each other. VPlus brings about a new method of communication for aspiring entrepeneurs.
